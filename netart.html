personal information index(pi²), website, 2019


We decided to do the Data as Self-Portrait option of the assignment. We created a fake online marketplace website where users can buy and sell people's personal data without their consent. We wanted to highlight the fact that huge companies like Apple, Microsoft, etc. are essentially doing this right now with no repercussions, which means that most people's personal information and data is basically being put up for sale to the highest bidder.


The link to the website is https://hectorm2017.github.io/Groupofgroups/
