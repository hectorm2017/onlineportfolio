the ancient wisdom of 2009, digital composite image, 2019


My group's concept for this piece was an emphasis on technology and our relationship to it, so for my portion of the assignment I decided to focus on how we treat technology and how quickly our innovations in technology become obsolete. In my piece, every piece of technology is from 10 years ago and I researched many of the trends of the late 2010's to try to be as accurate as possible. I started with the laptop in the center and continuously added more parts all centered around the laptop. I added the background last.
